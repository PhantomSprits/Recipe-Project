This Creamy Mac n Cheese has a prep time of 5 minutes and a cook time of 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Gemelli or Elbow Pasta
  • Unsalted Butter
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Whole Milk
  • Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.

  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the butter ina saucepan over medium heat until it begins to foam, then whisk in flour until smooth and cook 2 to 3 minutes. Whisk in the milk and seasoning mix and cook, stirring, until thickened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Remove from heat, stir in the cheese until melted, then add the cooked pasta, mixing thoroughly. Serve hot.
